Target Audience

The target audience of the Drizzle & Drip is men and women between the ages of 20-25. This site's sleek design, cool and aesthetic colors, and easy navigation makes it an excellent candidate for younger adults who are always on the go.

Persona 1

Sophie is in college and she has to walk to and from school, as well as to and from work. One day she wakes up late and has no idea what to wear because she doesn't know what the weather will be like today. She opens her phone and navigates to Drizzle & Drip. There she can quickly find the weather for the day and dress accordingly.

Sophie is in class and sees its snowing and wants to know how long it'll snow because she's planning on driving home this weekend.

Sophie has a group of friends on their way to the ice caves and clouds start to roll in, so they want a quick forecast on what the weather is doing.

Persona 2

Bryson is in class at the college, and his group of friends are trying to plan a snowshoeing trip. The trip details are all becoming spur of the moment, and so Bryson looks up the weather on Drizzle & Drip to quickly see if the weather will be good enough for him and his friends to go snowshoeing.

Bryson wants to go skiing, but wants to make sure there will be some fresh powder to make the experience even better.
