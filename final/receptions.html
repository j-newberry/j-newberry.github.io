Receptions

The Temple Inn & Suites has a number of services and locations for wedding receptions close to our temple areas. Each hotel has areas for receptions indoors, and outdoors! We would like to work with you for details pertaining to your big day! Set up and take-down fees are included in the reception package. Prices vary in certain locations.

If you wish to have a food service or catering, we offer that as well! We cater menus from our own hotel as well as local catering companies nearby.
