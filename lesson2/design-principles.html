Design Principles

CIT 230

White Space and Clean Design

See Jane here

This online clothing boutique called Jane demonstrates white space and clean design in it's simplistic and clean layout. This website has a nice, light color pallet, along with a sleek straight page layout. Each picture of product is also centered inside it's assigned space. These factors really draw the eye in, making it very appealing to look at.

Proximity

See Pinterest here

This website has great proximity, or spacing. The images are all the same distance apart, around all edges. This makes the mobile site app so appealing to the eye because you don't have to look all over at some really weird spacing, instead you're looking at very pleasantly spaced images. The spacing helps in terms of organization as well.

ALignment

See Twitter here

On Twitter, everything is in great alignment. From profile pictures on the left, to the edges of text, to the comment, like, retweet, and share buttons. Each tweet is the same distance apart, which plays an important part in alighnment. All around Twitter is an excellent example of alignment.
